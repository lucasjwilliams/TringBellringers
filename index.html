Welcome to Tring Bellringers

We ring the bells at St Peter & St Paul’s Church in the heart of Tring.

We are the bellringers of St Peter & St Paul’s Church, Tring. We ring for Sunday services, weddings, civic occasions and national events, and we are always pleased to welcome new ringers and visitors.

Bellringing is a unique mix of music, rhythm, teamwork and tradition. It is sociable, absorbing and surprisingly challenging, and you do not need to be musical or experienced to learn.

If you are curious, come and see what happens in the tower.
