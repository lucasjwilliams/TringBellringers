## Links

### Dove’s Guide — full tower details

Dove’s Guide provides technical details about the bells at Tring, including the number of bells, weights, tuning, inscriptions and other tower information.

[View Tring tower details on Dove’s Guide](https://dove.cccbr.org.uk/detail.php?tower=15131)

### Quarter peals and peals at Tring

You can find records of quarter peals and peals rung at Tring through BellBoard.

[View ringing performances at Tring](https://bb.ringingworld.co.uk/search?dove_tower=15131&length=q-or-p)

Learning the Ropes is a national scheme that supports new ringers as they learn bell handling, ringing skills and change ringing.
